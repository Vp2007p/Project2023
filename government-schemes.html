Government Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana is a crop insurance scheme launched by the Government of India to provide insurance coverage to the farmers in case of crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

The scheme is available to all farmers, and the premium rates are very low. The government pays the rest of the premium amount to the insurance companies.

The scheme has helped farmers to recover their losses and get back on their feet after crop failure.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana is an irrigation scheme launched by the Government of India to provide irrigation facilities to the farmers.

The scheme aims to improve the productivity of crops and ensure food security in the country. It also focuses on sustainable water management practices.

Under the scheme, the government provides financial assistance to the farmers for the development of irrigation facilities. The government also encourages the use of micro-irrigation techniques, which helps in water conservation.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana is an income support scheme launched by the Government of India to provide financial assistance to the farmers.

Under the scheme, the government provides an income support of Rs. 6000 per year to the farmers. The amount is transferred directly to the bank account of the farmers.

The scheme aims to provide financial stability to the farmers and ensure their well-being. The scheme has benefited many farmers across the country.
